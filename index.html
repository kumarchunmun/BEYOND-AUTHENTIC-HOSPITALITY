Catering Service

catering services provide on-site food and beverage solutions for guests, combining residential comfort with restaurant-style hospitality. Ranging from breakfast buffets to full-service meals, these services focus on convenience, offering tailored menus for meetings, events, or daily stay requirements

Manpower Solution

Manpower solution services are external HR services that source, screen, and provide temporary, contract, or permanent staff to businesses. They enable firms to quickly fill vacancies, manage seasonal hiring, and handle payroll/compliance, reducing recruitment time and administrative burdens.

Electrical HT & LT panel

Electrical HT (High Tension) and LT (Low Tension) panel services provide essential maintenance, testing, and repair to ensure industrial/commercial power systems remain safe and operational. Services include thermal scanning, cleaning, tightening connections, relay testing, and oil testing to prevent failures. Expert technicians also handle installation, retrofitting, and energy audits, reducing downtime.

Lift Management

Lift management services ensure that elevators (lifts) operate safely, efficiently, and reliably in residential and commercial buildings. These services include regular maintenance, repairs, inspections, and safety compliance checks to prevent breakdowns and extend the equipment's lifespan

DG Management

Diesel Generator (DG) Management Service ensures the reliable, safe, and efficient operation of standby power systems. It involves proactive maintenance, monitoring, and repair to guarantee power continuity during outages.

HVAC

Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning, referring to the systems that regulate indoor environmental comfort. They manage temperature (heating/cooling), humidity, and air purity using components like furnaces, heat pumps, filters, and ducts to ensure healthy, comfortable air
